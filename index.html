Tessa Coates: Lockdown and Two Smoking Barrels

Tessa is having an absolute time of it in lockdown, she's learnt to cook one dish, she's painted a wall with a copper effect paint she bought on Instagram and it looks dreadful. She'd love you to come and hang out with her for this hour of wildly elaborate anecdotes that may or may not be true. There'll be some science, some history, at least one impression and possibly about 45 minutes on the 1904 St Louis Olympics. It'll be nice.

    How do I reset my password?

    Step 1. Navigate to the login form by clicking on the Login button or on the content you wish to watch.

    Step 2. Click the Forgot Password link below the password field on the login form.

    Step 3. Enter your email address and click the Reset button.

    Step 4. A new window will pop up. Make sure to leave it open.

    Step 5. Check your email. You will receive a password reset token. If you don’t see it in your Inbox, make sure to check your Spam and Trash folders.

    Step 6. Copy the Token and paste it in the first field of the window that popped up previously.

    Step 7. Enter your new password in the second and third field.

    Step 8. Click Set

    If you do not receive a password reset token please contact us at support@inplayer.com.
